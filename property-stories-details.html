PROPERTY STORY TITLE 1

In every individual client that I assisted over the past 10 years, most of them have the same concerns when it comes to property upgrading and investment.


And it always boils down to these few concerns:


1. Fear of increasing loan liabilities.


2. Fear of choosing the wrong property.


3. Uncertainty involved in the decision-making process.


These concerns has kept them from moving forward which is understandable. I have spoken to many families over the years. I also have experienced it first hand when I did my own upgrading.
